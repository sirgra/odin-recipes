Caesar Salad Recipe

Picture of a fresh Caesar Salad

Caesar Salad is a classic dish known for its crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, and crunchy croutons.
The salad is typically topped with grilled chicken or shrimp, making it a hearty meal option.
The dressing is made from a blend of garlic, anchovies, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and Parmesan cheese, creating a rich and tangy flavor that complements the fresh greens.
The addition of croutons adds a satisfying crunch, while the Parmesan cheese provides a salty, nutty finish.
This salad is perfect as a light lunch or as a side dish for dinner, and it pairs well with a variety of main courses.
Whether enjoyed at a restaurant or made at home, Caesar Salad remains a favorite for its simplicity and delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Grilled chicken or shrimp
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Anchovies
  • Lemon juice
  • Dijon mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together minced garlic, anchovies, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and olive oil to create the dressing.
  3. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the chopped lettuce, grilled chicken or shrimp, and croutons.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all ingredients evenly.
  6. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of the salad.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
  8. Enjoy your delicious Caesar Salad!

Lasagna Recipe

Picture of a fresh slice of Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of cheese.
To begin, ground beef is sautéed with onions and garlic, then simmered with tomatoes, herbs, and a touch of red wine for depth of flavor.
A smooth béchamel sauce made from butter, flour, and milk adds a silky layer between the pasta and meat.
Grated Parmesan and mozzarella cheeses are sprinkled generously throughout each layer, creating a perfect balance of textures and flavors.

Once the layers are assembled in a baking dish—starting with sauce, then pasta, then béchamel and cheese—the lasagna is baked in the oven until bubbling and golden brown on top.
After resting for a few minutes to set, it slices easily into hearty portions. This comforting, satisfying meal pairs wonderfully with a simple green salad and a glass of red wine,
making it perfect for family dinners or casual gatherings with friends.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Red wine
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté chopped onions and minced garlic in olive oil until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, herbs, and red wine. Simmer for about 20 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to create a roux, cooking for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Gradually whisk in milk, cooking until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna sheets, then béchamel sauce, and a sprinkle of cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of béchamel and a generous topping of cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  11. Serve hot with a side salad and enjoy!
